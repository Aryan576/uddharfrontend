Our Story

We as a part of Uddhar (RESCUE- Meaning in English) we are an Non Govermental Organization (NGO) Based in INDIA that works for the welfare of animals and birds to provide then better Health quality and Lifestyle as our tag line says "Right to Live". We provide almost all services that any any animal need at a very low cost and some for free as we are an NGO we operate on the donations we get. so if you liked our work you can also help us making our community bigger and help animals on a bigger scale.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to reach to each and every corner of the world and provide the best quality service to every animal and make people aware to to take proper care of their pets and any animals near their surroundings.

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide ultimate professional Veterinary practice along with pet's health care that combines excellent healthcare delivers in a consistent, innovative, enjoyable, caring and pet-friendly way to provide unforgettable service and experience from us
